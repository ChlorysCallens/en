For me, art is a process where you create something. An image, idea or vision that you convey to the viewer. I mainly focus on the impression that images give me. The inspiration for my paintings comes from my environment. Daily scenes, conversations and passages, but also trips that have changed the world for me.

The studio where i'm painting in is in Sint Lucas Ghent. I'm currently studying in the Third year visual arts. The studio is located in Alexianenplein 1 in Ghent. For more studio images of the painting department, visit the Sint Lucas blog.

For questions and info please contact me on phone, e-mail of Facebook.
