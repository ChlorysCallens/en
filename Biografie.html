    Atmosphere
mobirise.com

For me, art is a process of creating something. An image, idea or vision that you convey to the viewer. This can be created from your own view, but it can also be seen from the perspective you think the viewer has. I focus especially on the impression that certain images can give me. The atmosphere they invoke or leave behind, both positive or negative. I work especially around the recalling and capturing of that atmosphere and the creation of a full image.


I find the inspiration for my paintings from my surroundings, daily scenes, conversations and passages, but also trips that have changed my own perspective. The studio where I paint, is located in Sint-Lucas, Ghent. I am currently studying in the second year of Fine Arts. The studio is located at Alexianenplein 1 in Ghent. For more studio images of the painting department, visit the Saint Lucas blog.
